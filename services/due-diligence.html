Due Diligence

Due Diligence investigations are conducted on companies, their partners and top management with a goal to determine if that company fit the criteria of the client’s ethics and compliance rules as well as their code of conduct.

These investigations focus on general compliance issues as well as inspection of public information sources to uncover or discover the existence of specific matters such as reputation, inappropriate business conduct, political ties and influence, money laundering, bribery, negative court rulings etc.
