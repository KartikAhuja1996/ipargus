Brand Protection

In todays' consumerist day and age, the brand value of a company is key to its profits. It takes years to set up a unique brand that appeals to consumers and becomes a leader in its sector of the economy. Various factors such as intensive research and development, smart marketing and, publicity contribute to building a brand's reputation and make it sought-after. A higher value of the brand also allows the company to charge more for its products. We as a team understand your business needs and tailor an efficient strategy to protect your brand from counterfeiters across the country. We work with clients to ensure that brand management and enforcement programs are in place before any marks are launched. Our proven and proactive anti-counterfeiting services and countermeasures solutions are designed to help clients counter counterfeiting. Our team knows that an effective anti-counterfeit program requires a solid working relationship with law enforcement authorities. We work with law enforcement to protect our clients against counterfeiters and enforce brand owners’ IP rights. Our team has organized investigations and raids that have resulted in the seizure of hundreds of thousands of counterfeit products. Our experts offer proactive approaches to help prevent counterfeiting and, in the event it does occur, discover the problem quickly. The team counsels clients on best practices and strategic risk reduction measures to prevent counterfeiting. This advice includes methods to secure products and packaging against counterfeiters and the development of programs to guard and monitor distribution channels. We are more interested in managing the efforts as a whole with the long term in mind, in other words, we are looking to be your brand’s solution partner.
